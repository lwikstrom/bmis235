Discover Spain

Spain, a country on Europe’s Iberian Peninsula, includes 17 autonomous regions with diverse geography and cultures. The capital city is Madrid, known for its royal palace and Prado museum, housing works by European masters.

Spain is renowned for its rich history, vibrant culture, and stunning landscapes. From the bustling streets of Barcelona to the serene beaches of Costa del Sol, Spain offers something for every traveler.
